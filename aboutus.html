Collective Retreats is proud to be the first and largest global outdoor hospitality brand to receive certified B-Corp status. Since our inception, our core values have been focused on continually creating positive social and environmental impact for the communities we serve. This certification represents a milestone in our commitment to connect people, build a greater awareness for the natural beauty that surrounds us and reinforce our deep-rooted belief that we can deliver unique luxury travel experiences that are more memorable and less extractive than traditional hotels.

Collective Retreats is a Public Benefit Corporation, a corporate structure which legally obligates us to consider the impact of our decisions on the environment, not just shareholders.
